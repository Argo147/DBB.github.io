About Us

We are an FTC team from the Derryfield School in Manchester, NH, driven by a passion for robotics and a commitment to fostering inclusivity and STEM engagement within our community. While the history of robotics at our school traces back to 2011, the establishment of our team, 12758 Binary Bots, took place in 2017. Our primary objective is to carry forward the legacy of excellence set by our school's robotics endeavors, while placing a strong emphasis on providing opportunities for all to participate in FTC and actively engage in the world of STEM.

About First Tech Challenge

FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is robotics competition where teams design, build, program, and operate their own robots to tackle a series of complex challenges presented each season. It is part of the larger FIRST program, which aims to "inspire young people to be science and technology leaders and innovators, by engaging them in exciting mentor-based programs that build science, engineering, and technology skills, that inspire innovation, and that foster well-rounded life capabilities including self-confidence, communication, and leadership."
